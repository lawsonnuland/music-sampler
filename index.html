The Black Eyed Peas are best characterized as artists that slowly diverged from their original sound until they were no longer recognizable. While they started out with good beats and rhythm, and a very unique and distinguishable sound, they eventually became completely unremarkable due to their use of synthesizers and auto tune, which diluted the quality of their music. Witness the slow degredation yourself by clicking on the albums below and taking a listen.
